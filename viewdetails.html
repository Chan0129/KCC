Basic Woodworking Techniques

Learn fundamental woodworking skills from our master craftsmen. Perfect for beginners interested in furniture making.

June 15, 2025
9:00 AM - 4:00 PM
KCC Main Showroom

About This Workshop

Join us for a comprehensive woodworking workshop designed for beginners who want to learn the fundamentals of furniture making. Our master craftsmen will guide you through essential techniques, safety practices, and creative approaches to woodworking.

This hands-on workshop will cover everything from selecting the right wood to finishing techniques that bring out the natural beauty of your creations. Whether you're looking to start a new hobby or enhance your DIY skills, this workshop provides the perfect foundation.

What You'll Learn

  • Understanding different types of wood and their properties
  • Essential woodworking tools and their proper usage
  • Basic joinery techniques for strong, durable furniture
  • Safety protocols in the workshop
  • Sanding and finishing techniques for professional results
  • Design principles for functional and beautiful furniture

What's Included

  • All necessary materials and tools
  • Expert instruction from master craftsmen
  • Take-home project (small wooden stool)
  • Workshop manual with techniques and tips
  • Certificate of completion
  • Light refreshments and lunch

Who Should Attend

This workshop is perfect for absolute beginners with no prior woodworking experience, DIY enthusiasts looking to improve their skills, homeowners interested in furniture repair and restoration, and anyone curious about the craft of woodworking.

What to Bring

Just bring your enthusiasm and willingness to learn! We provide all tools, materials, and safety equipment. We recommend wearing comfortable clothing that you don't mind getting dusty, and closed-toe shoes for safety.
